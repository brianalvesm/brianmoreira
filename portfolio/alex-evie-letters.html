Alex & Evie's Letters

This is a set of illustrations of Alex (Cake’s owner), Evie and other characters entangled in a story. The art is intended to accompany a series of letters where the story unfolds. The client requested a collection of 62 unique illustrations of a variety of situations, places and scenarios.

“Future Me” 2024 XPPen Global Drawing Contest

For the XPPen 2024 Contest, I was tasked to imagine my future. Usually when we wonder about what the future is going to be, we often picture a very silver, metalic, sometimes dystopian future, full with robots and androids. So I decided that, for my future, I want it to be brighter, sunny and full of life. I imagined me, my future wife and our cat, enjoying nature with our future daughter. I have my painting supplies with me so I can do some traditional artwork too, because we can't just use screens all the time. Oh, and jetpacks! In the future, we must have jetpacks.

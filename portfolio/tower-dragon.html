Tower Dragon

This one took a while. I drew the line art way back in july 2024, and only now in january of 2026 it is finally done! Perhaps the only good thing about taking a year and a half to finish an artwork, is that you can see your progress and your skills improving over time. Because I really figure out a lot ever since and the result now is much better.

January, 2026

Development Notes

The line art was done by hand on paper. As you can see, the original drawing doesn’t have the little brave hero. He only came to be towards the end of the painting process, when I looked at all this space on the bottom and felt it needed something. I think it really brought the art together. Before, the focal point was just the dragon. Now, there’s a line the eyes can travel through the whole picture.

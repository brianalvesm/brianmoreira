The DIGI POGS Collection

Remember pogs!? Or Tazos, as they were called here, was a big hit in Brazil during the 90's and early 2000's. Well, I loved growing up with them and collecting as many as I could. And this is a project of mine I'm calling DIGI POGS, a collection of fan arts illustrated by me, of characters I like from many different animations, comics and games. Click on each one to see the other side of the pog!
